去中心化的协议智能合约

Decentralized Agreements with Smart Contracts

区块链可以创建确定性的，防篡改的协议都知道叫做智能合约

Blockchains enable the creation of deterministic, tamper-proof agreements known as smart contracts.

设想其为传统的合法合约，但是其条款是写入代码中和存储在区块链上的

Think of them as traditional legal contracts, but with their terms written in code and stored on the blockchain

这些合约自动的执行当他们的预定义条件被满足，制作它们透明，不可变的和高效的

These contracts automatically execute when their predefined conditions are met, making them transparent, immutable, and highly efficient.

例如: 自动保险: 保险策略被书面化后当做一个智能合约

Example: Automated Insurance: An insurance policy can be written as a smart contract.

如果一个可验证的事件产生，这么当一个主要洪水报告通过一个可信的数据源，发生，这智能合约能够自动的执行去赔付投保人。

If a verifiable event, such as a major flood reported by a trusted data source, occurs, the smart contract can automatically execute the payout to the policyholder.

这种可确定性的处理消除管理的开销，

This deterministic process eliminates administrative overhead

必要的文件声明和信任在保险公司的审批之上，确保一个快速和公平的解决掉

the need to file a claim, and the reliance on an insurance company’s approval, ensuring a fast and fair settlement.
