Blockchains enable the creation of deterministic, tamper-proof agreements known as smart contracts. 
#### 设想其为传统的合法合约，但是其条款是写入代码中和存储在区块链上的
Think of them as traditional legal contracts, but with their terms written in code and stored on the blockchain
